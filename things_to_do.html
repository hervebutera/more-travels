Learn about the things to do in Rwanda.

With most travelers thinking of travelling to Rwanda, one may wonder what to put on his top list of places to visit. More travels helps you explore the top destinations Rwanda has. Explore the top destinations!

Things to do

Safari and tours

The wildlife safari offers you a chance to catch a glimpse at Rwanda’s distinct wildlife species including the rare mountain gorillas, chimpanzees and the big game. It takes you to Volcanoes National Park for gorilla trekking, wildlife exploration in Akagera National Parks and chimpanzee tracking in Nyungwe Forest National Park.

Adventure and activities

With our experienced leaders, and high quality equipment, we run the best Adventure Activities in Rwanda. From short excursions to multi-day journeys, many of which are suitable for children and families, we'll help you to discover the Rwandan countryside and connect with local people.

Business trips

More travels understands that the relationship between organizations and their traveling employees is changing. Your employees need business travel agents who can help them stay productive, get the best prices, and make travel easier for them.

Comunity based tourism

Rwanda community tourism engages the locals living around the countries attractions to the tourism in the country. The community people have a chance to showcase culture with tourists. More travels helps you in all the needs to have a better experience.

Cultural tourism

However you journey around Rwanda you will meet many amazing people. However, we always like to include cultural insights into all our tours because these experiences bring a vibrant, exciting country to life and often become the most meaningful experiences that are, for many, highlights of their visit.

City tours

The architecture and culture collide on a fascinating, guided Kigali city tour. The vibrancy of Kigali is beautifully showcased and the rapidly changing façade of the city is due to the numerous visitors and investors who are attracted to what has been hailed as one of the friendliest and safest cities in Africa.

Educative trips

We assist arrange a group tour or school tours, school-cultural trips, community-based school safari for high school students, college and universities. With our experienced experts, we can help plan for you to best school and group tour while considering your wallet size and objective of your travel.

Entertainment trips

Entertainment travel is unique. We strive to meet your high expectations, whether you’re handling film and TV productions, celebrity travel, band and broadway show tours or on-location fashion shoots.
